Gymbro Airfried Chicken strips

Description

Delicious and easy to make chicken strips bursting with flavour and juiciness. Are you even a gym bro if you don't own an air fryer?

Ingredients

  • 250g Chicken breast
  • Moroccan Seasoning (careful, this has seed oils!!!)
  • Red Chilli paste

Instructions

  1. Slice your chicken breast into 10cm long strips
  2. Chuck your chicken strips into a bowl and lather it with that moroccan seasoning and that tasty red chilli paste
  3. Leave for 15 minutes to marinade
  4. Throw that bad boy into the air fryer at 180C for 15 minutes
  5. Play a quick game of Valorant spike rush
  6. Serve with any low sugar, no GMO, organic sauce of your liking. (I recommend sriracha!)
